Support Center

We're here to assist you with any questions or concerns

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place a bid on an auction?

To place a bid, navigate to the Active Auctions page from the Buyer Menu. Select the item you're interested in and click the "Place Bid" button. Enter your maximum bid amount and confirm. You'll receive notifications if you're outbid.

What is your authentication process?

Every item undergoes a rigorous multi-point authentication by our team of luxury goods experts. We examine materials, craftsmanship, serial numbers, and provenance documentation. Items that don't pass authentication are never listed on our platform.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days for domestic orders and 10-14 business days for international orders. Express shipping options are available at checkout. All items are fully insured and require signature confirmation.

Can I consign my luxury items with SERIAE?

Yes! Visit the Product Registration page from the Seller Menu to begin the consignment process. Our team will review your submission and contact you within 48 hours with a valuation and consignment terms.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 14-day return window for all purchases. Items must be returned in their original condition with all packaging and documentation. Auction purchases are final and non-refundable unless the item is materially different from its description.

How do I upgrade to Premium membership?

Premium membership is available through invitation only. Members who complete three or more transactions or consign items valued over £10,000 are automatically considered for Premium status, which includes early access to new arrivals, private showroom appointments, and reduced commission fees.
